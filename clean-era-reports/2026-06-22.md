## CLV — closing-line value (R1.1)

CLV = market move toward our side after we bet (our odds vs closing odds). **This is the leading indicator** — it gives signal on every bet immediately, unlike W/L. Sustained positive CLV on staked bets is the durable proof the edge is real, not luck. Rows without a closing snapshot are excluded from CLV counts.
